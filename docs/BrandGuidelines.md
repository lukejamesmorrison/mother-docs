## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
- Use official brand colors for Mother-related content
- Credit Mother Core using the [Powered By Mother](#powered-by-mother-badge) badge on your projects and documentation

### Don'ts
- Don't modify the logo colors or proportions
- Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce visibility
- Don't stretch or distort any brand assets
